 About Us

**Bin Naqshe Kadam** is an India-based travel company that specializes in creating unique travel experiences for adventurous individuals, including teenagers, students, and solo travelers. The company offers travel packages where participants can explore new destinations with a group of like-minded, unknown individuals. These journeys are designed to foster a sense of discovery, personal growth, and connection, allowing travelers to step out of their comfort zones and experience the world in a completely new way. By encouraging interaction among strangers, Bin Nakshe Kadam aims to create unforgettable memories and lifelong friendships through the shared joy of exploration.

Our Story

  • Bin Naqshe Kadam was founded in 2018 with an aim to change how the world sees and experiences travel.

  • On its inception, we did not know how people would react to such a concept where they know next to nothing about a trip- where they are headed, means of travel or accommodation. People aren't quick to trust someone blindly. One of our travellers merrily calls this concept "kidnapping with consent".

  • Bin Naqshe Kadam aims to disrupt the travel ecosystem comprising of the current traditional travel companies making the world of travel more and more mechanical, and systematic.

  • We are here to CHANGE THIS.

  • With Bin Naqshe Kadam, there is no itinerary, no plans, no hurry to reach or leave from anywhere. We don't have any lists, learn to travel slow with us, and to be in the present moment.

  • None of the travellers travelling with us know where they are going. 'Travel without knowing where you are travelling to' is our motto.

  • If you ever travel with Bin Naqshe Kadam or attend any of our events, a part of BNK stays with you for life.

  • BNK is not just about travelling. It is a way of life.

  • For us, it does not start and end with a trip, it is something that resides in you, at all times. We believe in building lifetime relationships with our vendors, and also travellers.

  • We have organized over 500+ trips/ events and experiences, hosting 10,000+ new-age travellers and have built a community of over 50,000+ travellers.

  • We are currently bootstrapped with 100% owned equity, and are continuously working hard with a small in-house team to build new concepts and emerge as a stronger and better community of new- age travellers

  • Join our journey, as we build the biggest, strongest, meaningful, and impactful travel community, proudly from INIA.
